About Us

First Year Involvement connects students to campus wide opportunities and resources through different events and initiatives. By bringing first-years together throughout year, this facet of the USC continues to promote school pride and foster leadership throughout the academic season.

What is Our Goal?

Would you like to learn about leadership opportunities tailored to first years and network with like minded students? The USC FYI is hosting the inagural Empower Yourself Conference, with all you need to know about various club, corporate, and leadership positions being offered next year. There will be free refreshments, a LinkedIn photobooth, and Leadership Starter Kits. Keynote speaker will be Morgan Rosenberg, current Vice-President of AIESEC.

Event FAQs

Is there a cost for this event?

Nope!

This event is completely free, presented to you by the USC FYIC.

University Community Center

Is this conference exclusive to first years?

Nope!

We target first-years seeking to learn more about the extracurricular and leadership oppertunities availible on campus, but everyone is welcome!
